Mining is one of the core skills in Hypixel Skyblock. It goes up to level 60

Spelunker

Spelunker is the skill's ability which is exclusively upgraded by increasing your Mining Skill. For every level of Spelunker you gain an additional +4 Mining Fortune, which means at Mining level 50, you gain +200 Mining Fortune, and at Mining level 60, you gain +240 Mining Fortune.
