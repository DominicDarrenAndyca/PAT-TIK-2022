Combat is one of the core player Skills players can level up as they earn Combat XP. The max level Combat is 60 and you start at level 0.

Warrior

Warrior is the skill's ability which is exclusively upgraded by increasing your Combat Skill. For every level of Warrior you gain an additional 4% additional damage against monsters. Above Combat 50, you gain 1% additional damage against monsters. Warrior is additive with Enchants found on Weapons.

Extra Stuff

  1. Leveling up farming will grant crit chance, 4%/5% more damage against mobs per level, and access to certain armor sets
  2. At combat level 1, you get access to The Spider Den.
  3. At combat level 5, you get access to The Wardrobe.
  4. At combat level 6, you get Auto-Pickup
  5. At combat level 12, you get access to The End
  6. At combat level 24, you get access to The Crimson Isle
  7. Combat 50 will give you 25 Crit Chance in total
  8. Combat 60 will give you 30 Crit Chance in total
  9. Best way to grind for combat xp is killing Revenants or Ghosts with a Wolf Pet, Combat XP III potion, Booster Cookie, Cultivating X and killing all tier 4 slayer bosses to get a 25% boost
