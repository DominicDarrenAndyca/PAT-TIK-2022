Enchanting is one of the 12 Skills players can level up as they earn Enchanting EXP. The max level enchanting is 60 and you start at level 0.

Conjurer

Conjurer is the skill's ability which is exclusively upgraded by increasing your Enchanting Skill. For every level of Conjurer you gain an additional +4% Extra Experience Orbs from any source. If you were Enchanting level 25, you would gain +100% Extra Experience Orbs.

Enchanting XP Gain

Enchanting EXP is primarily earned by doing Experiments in the Experimentation Table. You can also gain up to 500,000 Enchanting EXP from enchanting regular books in the Enchanting Table.

Extra Stuff

  1. Leveling up enchanting will grant ability damage, intelegence, and access to new enchants
  2. Enchanting 60 will give you 30 ability damage, 106 intelegence, and access to 81 enchants
  3. Best way to grind for enchanting xp is using the experimentation table with a Guardian Pet, Bosster Cookie, and a Enchanting XP III Potion
