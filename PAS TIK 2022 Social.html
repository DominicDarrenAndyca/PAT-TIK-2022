Social Skill is one of the cosmetic player Skills players can level up as they earn Social XP, it is tied to your profile and shared between co-op members. The max Social level is 25 and you start at level 0.

Currently, the Social Skill only provides cosmetic changes to players with a small amount of Coins.

Levelling

Social XP is primarily earned by people visiting your island, and hanging out with guests.

Social XP Gain

  1. Players earn 10 Social EXP per unique guest.
  2. Players earn 1 EXP per minute when hanging out with guests.
  3. Players earn 1 EXP per minute when guesting other people's islands.
