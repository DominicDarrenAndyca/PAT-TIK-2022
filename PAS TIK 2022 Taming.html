Taming is one of the 12 Skills players can level up as they earn Taming XP. The max Taming level is 50 and you start at level 0.

Zoologist

Zoologist is the skill's ability which is exclusively upgraded by increasing your Taming Skill. For every level of Zoologist you gain an additional +1% Extra Pet EXP. If you were Taming level 25, you would gain +25% Extra Pet EXP. Additionally you also gain +1 Pet Luck per Taming level.

Levelling

Taming XP is earned by gaining other types of Skill XP while having a Pets active.

Extra Stuff

  1. Leveling up taming will grant Pet Luck
  2. Taming 50 will give you 50 Pet Luck
  3. Best way to grind for taming xp is to have a Booster Cookie, a skill boost pet item on your pet, and using a mythic enderman or a legendary tarantula pet
  4. However, this skill levels up by itself. So you don't need to actively grind for taming xp
