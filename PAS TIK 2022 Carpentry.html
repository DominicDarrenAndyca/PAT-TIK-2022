Carpentry is one of the two cosmetic player Skills players can level up when they craft items. The Carpentry goes up to 50 levels, starting at level 0.

Unlocking

This skill can be unlocked after talking to the Carpenter and giving him 64 Wool. Once the quest is completed, the Carpenter will grant the player the recipe for the Carpentry Table.

Leveling

You gain Carpentry XP by crafting items. Most items when crafted will give Carpentry XP except for certain vanilla items.

The only way to increase your Carpentry XP gained is by having a Hypixel Rank. Higher ranks will increase your XP more.
