Dungeoneering is one of the 12 Skills players can level up as they earn Dungeoneering XP.

Dungeoneering is the main skill brought forth in the Dungeons update.

Dungeoneering has several sub-skills in it; one for each dungeon(Only Catacombs right now), and one for each Class:

  1. Healer
  2. Mage
  3. Berserker
  4. Archer
  5. Tank

Dungeons

Each Dungeon has its own Dungeoneering Level. Increasing the Dungeoneering level increases the stats of Dungeon Items in the respective Dungeon. At Dungeoneering 50, items are increased by 465%.

Dungeoneering XP Gain

Dungeoneering XP can be earned by participating in or completing Dungeon runs. Currently, there is only one type of Dungeon in SkyBlock, that being the Catacombs. However, if more do get released, you'll gain Dungeoneering XP for the specific type of Dungeon that you are playing in.
