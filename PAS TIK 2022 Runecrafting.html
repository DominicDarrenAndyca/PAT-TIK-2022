Runecrafting is a cosmetic based Skill. The Runecrafting level goes up to 25 levels, starting at level 0. If you are a default rank the max you can have for Runecrafting is level 3.

Leveling

You can level Runecrafting from killing Runic Mobs which have a Purple Name Tag. You can also level up from Combining Runes or fusing them with another item at a Runic Pedestal in the hub after talking to Dusk. Depending on the tier and the rarity of the Rune you can get different amounts of Runecrafting xp.

Rewards

Gain access to new runes

Maximizing XP Gain

The only way to increase your Carpentry XP gained is by having a Hypixel Rank. Higher ranks will increase your XP more.
