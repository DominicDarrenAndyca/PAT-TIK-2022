Farming is one of the main skills in skyblock and is leveled up by harvesting crops or killing farm animals.

Jacob's Farming Contest

Jacob's Farming Contest is an Event that takes place every 3 SkyBlock Days and lasts for 20 minutes. During the event, players have a choice of 3 crops to farm (which are picked at random), having to increase their Collection while competing against other players.

The players who increase their Collection the most within the time limit are rewarded with Medals.

Bracket % Required Tickets Other Rewards
None Just participation 1 None
Bronze Top 60% 10 Turbo-Crop (Crop that you participated in)
Silver Top 25% 15 Turbo-Crop (Crop that you participated in)
Gold Top 5% 25 Turbo-Crop (Crop that you participated in)

Anita's Shop

Jacob's Tickets are Miscellaneous Items that can be traded at the Anita NPC in the Farmhouse for crop specific farming tools (Pumpkin Dicer, Melon Dicer, etc.), items that can help you build farms on your island (Prismapump, Hoe Of Greater Tilling, etc.), increase in crop yield percentage and Farming level cap (Up to LX). They are also used to craft second and third tiers of Mathematical Hoe Blueprint hoes.


Turbo-Crop books are Enchantments that can be put onto your farming tools and will grant you +5 Farming Fortune per tier (Up to V) for the specific type of crop. Tier IV book requires a Bronze medal to be obtained in that type of crop, while Tier V requires a Silver one.

Extra Stuff

  1. Leveling up farming will grant health, coins, and farming fortune.
  2. At farming level 1, you get access to The Barn.
  3. At farming level 2, you get access to The Mushroom Desert.
  4. And at farming 10, you get access to Jacob's Farming Contest
  5. Farming 50 will give you 192 health in total
  6. Farming 60 will give you 242 health in total
  7. Best way to grind for farming xp is farming pumpkins with a Pumpkin Dicer or sugarcane with a Turing Sugarcane Hoe with a Rabbit Pet, Farming XP III potion, Booster Cookie, Cultivating X and the Blessed Reforge
