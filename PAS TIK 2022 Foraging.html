Foraging is one of the core player Skills players can level up as they earn Foraging XP. The max level Foraging is 50 and you start at level 0.

Logger

Logger is the skill's ability which is exclusively upgraded by increasing your Foraging Skill. For every level of Logger you gain an additional +4 Foraging Fortune. If you were Foraging level 25, you would gain +100 Foraging Fortune.

Extra Stuff

  1. Leveling up farming will grant strength
  2. At foraging level 1, you get access to The Birch Park.
  3. At foraging level 2, you get access to The Spruce Woods.
  4. At foraging level 3, you get access to The Dark Thicket
  5. At foraging level 4, you get access to The Savana Woodland
  6. At foraging level 5, you get access to The Jungle Island
  7. Combat 50 will give you 75 strength in total
  8. Best way to grind for foraging xp is cutting down dark oak logs at The Dark Thicket with a Treecapitator, an Ocelot or Monkey Pet, Foraging XP III potion, Booster Cookie, and The Toil Reforge
